# The Models of our Case Study {#the-models-of-our-case-study .title}




In our case study we construct three models of different systems:

1.  The model of the business system describes passenger services,
    meaning the business surroundings of the IT system. It deals with
    business processes, passengers, business partners, employees, etc.
    We discuss this model in *[Modeling Business
    Systems](/uml/modeling-business-systems)*.

2.  The model of the IT system explains the IT system that was built for
    passenger services. The model of the passenger service business
    system serves as the foundation for the model of the IT system. We
    discuss this model in *[Modeling IT
    Systems](/uml/modeling-it-systems)*.

3.  The model of system integration describes integration into the
    environment, especially gateways to the outside world. Here also,
    the model of the passenger service business system serves as the
    foundation. This model is discussed in *[Modeling for System
    Integration](/uml/modeling-for-system-integration)*:

<h1 id="the-models-of-our-case-study-1" class="title">The Models of our
Case Study</h1>
<div class="banner prom" data-id="CarbonAds UML"
data-creative-id="carbon-image" data-position="content_bottom">

</div>
<p>In our case study we construct three models of different systems:</p>
<ol>
<li><p>The model of the business system describes passenger services,
meaning the business surroundings of the IT system. It deals with
business processes, passengers, business partners, employees, etc. We
discuss this model in <em><a
href="/uml/modeling-business-systems">Modeling Business
Systems</a></em>.</p></li>
<li><p>The model of the IT system explains the IT system that was built
for passenger services. The model of the passenger service business
system serves as the foundation for the model of the IT system. We
discuss this model in <em><a href="/uml/modeling-it-systems">Modeling IT
Systems</a></em>.</p></li>
<li><p>The model of system integration describes integration into the
environment, especially gateways to the outside world. Here also, the
model of the passenger service business system serves as the foundation.
This model is discussed in <em><a
href="/uml/modeling-for-system-integration">Modeling for System
Integration</a></em>:</p></li>
</ol>
